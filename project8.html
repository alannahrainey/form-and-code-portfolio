    Reflections

    When creating this game, using objects to program Mission: Juniper made the process more concise and effective. I found inspiration for the design of my autobiographical game from my kitten, Juniper. I drew from the mundanity of feeding her everyday as my inspiration and accomplishing the narration of the game by creating a win or lose scenario: Juniper is fed or she is hungry. I enjoyed making something that was a personal anecdote.
